My interest in cybersecurity started when I read about a major data breach that affected millions of people. I realized that behind every hack is a human story—someone's privacy violated, someone's trust broken. Technology should empower people, not expose them. I want to be part of the solution, building systems that protect rather than compromise. The cat-and-mouse game between attackers and defenders fascinates me, and I'm eager to learn how to think like both to build better defenses.
